The Vision

Clothed
in the Call

Prophets was born in a rainy locker room prayer after a state championship loss. The founder knelt alone on a wet concrete floor, still in full pads, and heard something: "You were set apart before you were formed." That night, a brand was born — not from ambition, but from obedience.

We believe athletes are missionaries. Their courts, fields, and tracks are mission fields. The stadium is a pulpit. The locker room is a sanctuary. And the gear you wear is a declaration of whose you are.

"Set apart before birth. Unbreakable in the field."

— PROPHETS BRAND MANIFESTO
The Pillars

Why We Exist

⚔
Servant Leadership
The greatest among you shall be your servant. Every role in our organization, every product we release, every event we host begins with one question: who does this serve? We lead from the bottom, not the top.
Mark 10:45 — "For even the Son of Man did not come to be served, but to serve."
◈
10% to Frontline Missions
Non-negotiable. Every order — regardless of size — sends 10% to verified frontline mission partners. We don't separate commerce from covenant. The harvest funds the harvest.
Malachi 3:10 — "Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse."
✦
The Prophetic Insight
Every garment carries a scripture and a Prophetic Insight — a hand-crafted, faith-based message connecting the physical garment to a spiritual truth. You're not just wearing clothing. You're wearing a word.
Hebrews 4:12 — "For the word of God is alive and active."
⬡
Community & Tournament
Our Summer Sabbath tournaments are mission fields. We hire officials, coordinate games, arrange teams — and infuse every moment with the culture of Servant Leadership. Sports as ministry. Competition as communion.
Proverbs 27:17 — "Iron sharpens iron, so one man sharpens another."

Event Coordination

Servant Leadership in action. Every detail serves the athlete, the family, and the game.

DUTY 01
Hiring & Officials
  • Post referee and scorekeeper listings
  • Credential verification for all staff
  • Pay schedules and role assignments
  • Conflict of interest screening
  • Day-of briefing and prayer

DUTY 02

  • Single and double elimination brackets
